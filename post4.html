Human-Centered AI Design

Keeping the Student at the Heart of Tech

Efficiency vs. Meaning

This post discusses the "Human-Centered" approach to AI writing. While AI can draft 1,000 words in seconds, it lacks the lived experience of a human student. Educators are using AI to automate the tedious parts of writing (like bibliography formatting) so students can focus on critical thinking.
