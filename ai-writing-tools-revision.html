The New Writing Coach

Gemini + Khan Academy Collaboration

AI as a Pedagogical Partner

In 2026, the focus of AI in education has shifted from "answering" to "coaching." The partnership between Google and Khan Academy has birthed a tool that uses Gemini to guide students through the revision process.

Instead of rewriting a paragraph for a student, the AI asks: "Your thesis is strong, but how could you provide more evidence in the second sentence?" This keeps the student in the driver's seat.

Post 2: ChatGPT Prism

Prism: The Scholar's Workspace

OpenAI's New Academic Interface

Collaborative Academic Writing

ChatGPT Prism is a native workspace designed specifically for research and long-form writing. It allows students to upload their primary sources and work alongside the AI to organize citations and improve narrative flow.

The "Revision Mode" highlights sections where the AI suggests more "active voice" or better transitions, making it a powerful tool for educational writing improvement.

Post 5: Drafting vs. Revision

The Drafting/Revision Split

Modern Strategies for University Students

A New Academic Workflow

University of Washington researchers have identified a "Two-Step" method for AI in writing. Students use ChatGPT for Brainstorming (Front-end) and Gemini for Revision (Back-end), but the "Drafting" (Middle) remains a human-driven process to ensure authentic skill development.
