Changing the Educational Narrative

Ending the Era of "AI Catching"

Forbes Education reports that 2026 is the year schools officially pivoted. Rather than trying to "catch" AI use, educators are teaching AI Fluency. Students are being graded on how they use tools like Gemini to refine their arguments and check for logical fallacies.
