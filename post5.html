A New Academic Workflow

University of Washington researchers have identified a "Two-Step" method for AI in writing. Students use ChatGPT for Brainstorming (Front-end) and Gemini for Revision (Back-end), but the "Drafting" (Middle) remains a human-driven process to ensure authentic skill development.
